Services

Exhibition Islam offers a range of exciting services making it easier for you organise your events. All we need to know are your ambitions for your event, and our experienced team will help you deliver your ideal event.

Exhibition Islam offers a broad range of services that are affordable, easy to access and delivered to the highest standard. Museums

and galleries

The Exhibition Islam Museum Collection boasts a comprehensive collection of ancient manuscripts from across the world, ranging from China to Spain and spanning over 1400 years - the entire period of Islamic civilisation.

Mint

The Exhibition Islam Coin collection showcases exquisite Islamic coinage which represents Muslim interaction with Europe for over a Millennium. Coin collections from a range a regions display just how prolific adaptable Muslim communities were to new realities.
